* The bad side of Jupyter notebook
    * Lack of history
    * Difficult to test
    * Mutable document
    * Hard to parameterize
    * Live collaboration

### Filling the Gaps - papermill
* Things to preserve
    * results linked to code
    * good visuals
    * easy to share
* Things to improve
    * not versioned
    * mutable state
    * templating

### New Users & Extensions
* Easy to extend
    * They're all component
